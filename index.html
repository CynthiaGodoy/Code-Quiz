Code Quiz

How to Play:

1. Click on the start/continue button to begin playing.

2. You will have 75 seconds on the timer.

3. For each question you get wrong, additional time will be deducted.

4. If you run out of time, the game is over.

5. If you complete the quiz you will recieve a score.

6. Add your initials to record your score.

7. You may replay or quit the quiz.
